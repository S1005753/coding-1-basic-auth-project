=========================
APP PLANNING & DESIGN
=========================

1. PROJECT IDEA
-------------------------
- What is your app called? Hoppin'
- What type of app is it? (journal, listings, notes, etc.) Fitness log
- What will users use your app for? To track their progress and find new excersises 

2. DATA MODEL (WHAT ARE YOU STORING?)
-------------------------
- What is a single “entry” in your app? The log-in page
- What fields will each entry have? (title, content, date, etc.) A title saying log in. Spaces to enter information for username and password. And a space to enter the register page
- How will entries be connected to a user? They can enter information

3. CORE FEATURES (CRUD)
-------------------------
- What can users CREATE? A username and password. What they did for the day. 
- What will they see on the main page (READ)? A title. Have you been hoppin' it? A record of their activites for the week.
- What can users EDIT (UPDATE)? The record. Their account if they wish to (Password or new account)
- What can users DELETE? Their account. Delete some past records if they need to.

4. USER FLOW
-------------------------
- What happens after a user logs in? They will be met with the option to create a log (how many days, possible goal, etc)
- What pages/routes will your app need? (dashboard, create, edit, etc.) Log in, register, choosing log style, the actual log.
- Describe a typical user experience from login → using your app. (Ready to start Hoppin' it?) - Log in or register - They can either choose a record type or go straight to their log - They can update their log

5. USER EXPERIENCE (UX)
-------------------------
- How should your app look and feel? Friendly, froggy, nice and healthy
- How will users navigate between pages? Buttons, like a log out button, account button.
- How will entries be displayed (list, cards, etc.)? A mix of list and cards, possibly a chart

6. OPTIONAL FEATURES
-------------------------
- What extra features might you add? (search, tags, sorting, etc.) To be decided.
